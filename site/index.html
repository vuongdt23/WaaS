Endpoints

  • GET /api/facts.jsonall facts
  • GET /api/facts/{id}.jsonone fact
  • GET /api/random.jsonrandom (rotates per deploy)
  • GET /api/fact-of-the-day/{MM-DD}.jsondeterministic by date
  • GET /api/species.jsonspecies index
  • GET /api/species/{slug}.jsonspecies + its facts
  • GET /api/categories.jsoncategory list
  • GET /api/index.jsonmeta
